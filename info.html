WingIt
Android App
Sept 2016 - Present
1 Summary

An Android app that allows users to experience spontaneous adventures anywhere, anytime. Creates a trip itinerary on the go, instead of planning the entire trip beforehand. Built with Google Maps API and a Firebase database. Version 2.0 on Google Play Store.
